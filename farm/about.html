About Our Farm

Rooted in tradition. Grown with care. Delivered with honesty.

Our Story

Mathiralamman Organic Farm is located in Annur and is rooted in traditional farming practices passed down through generations. Our focus has always been on growing healthy, chemical-free produce while respecting the land and the seasons.

We believe that good food starts with good soil. By using organic methods and careful crop planning, we ensure that our produce remains fresh, nutritious, and sustainably grown.

What We Grow

Our farm produces a variety of seasonal vegetables, coconuts, and limited fruits depending on the harvest cycle.
